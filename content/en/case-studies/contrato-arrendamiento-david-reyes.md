::CaseStudyDetailContent
---
challenge: "Our client David Reyes owns several apartments in Oaxaca that he rents out. However, more and more tenants were requesting a formal lease contract, and David didn't know how to prepare a contract that would protect his interests and adapt to the particularities of his patrimonial situation, since he had different types of properties with different needs."

strategy: "After consulting with David, we understood that he needed personalized contracts adapted to each type of property. We developed a comprehensive approach that included preparing different lease contract templates considering the specifics of each property - from short-term rentals to long-term ones, with different payment and maintenance terms."

outcome: "Thanks to our approach, David received personalized lease contracts that fully meet his needs and protect his interests as an owner. Each contract was adapted to the specific situation: some include conditions for tenants with pets, others have special provisions for utility payment arrangements. Today, David confidently rents his apartments, knowing that his legal interests are well protected."
