::CaseStudyDetailContent
---
challenge: "Our client Rosa Reyes had lent a significant amount of money to a trusted person, but due to the existing friendship, she did not request any document to support the loan, such as a promissory note or contract. Over time, the person refused to return the money, and without any document, it seemed difficult to demonstrate the existence of the debt."

strategy: "We designed a legal strategy that included the preparation of testimonial evidence and the collection of circumstantial evidence that could prove the existence of the loan. We filed a civil lawsuit for commercial loan, basing our position on good faith and the principles of Mexican civil law that recognize obligations derived from trust relationships."

outcome: "Thanks to our comprehensive legal strategy, which included the presentation of testimonial evidence and complementary documentary evidence, the court determined that there was an obligation to return the money. A favorable judgment was issued for our client, and today Rosa Reyes has her money back, demonstrating that even without formal documents, it is possible to recover credits when there is a well-structured legal strategy."
