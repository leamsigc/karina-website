::CaseStudyDetailContent
---
challenge: "A commercial plaza in Ocotlán received property tax bills with a 400% increase from the previous year, derived from a supposed 'cadastral update' performed by the municipality without prior notice or technical justification, threatening the financial viability of the tenants."

strategy: "We filed a Nullification Lawsuit before the Administrative Justice Court of the State of Oaxaca. We demonstrated through valuation expert reports that the municipal authority illegally and retroactively applied land and construction value tables, violating the principles of proportionality and tax equity."

outcome: "The Court issued a judgment declaring the nullity of the excessive charges. The municipal authority was ordered to issue new property tax bills applying the correct values, resulting in a 75% reduction of the originally demanded amount, protecting the company's finances."
