::CaseStudyDetailContent
---
challenge: "Our client Rubén Sánchez lost his debit card and, although he reported it immediately, days later he discovered that various unauthorized charges were made to his account. The bank initially refused to refund the charges, arguing that the deadlines for dispute had already passed, leaving Rubén in a situation of economic vulnerability."

strategy: "We analyzed the case in detail and documented all charges made after the loss report. We filed a formal claim with the bank based on the Consumer Protection and Defense Law and Bank of Mexico provisions on debit card operations. We demonstrated that the charges were made without authorization and after the loss report."

outcome: "Thanks to our intervention and knowledge of applicable regulations, the bank proceeded to fully refund the unauthorized charges. Rubén Sánchez received his refund check, recovering all the money that had been fraudulently charged to him. Today he has the peace of mind of knowing that his consumer rights were respected."
