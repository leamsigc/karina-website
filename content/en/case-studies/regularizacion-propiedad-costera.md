::CaseStudyDetailContent
---
challenge: "An investor group acquired possession rights to a high-value beachfront property in Puerto Escondido. However, they lacked public deeds, preventing them from obtaining construction permits, bank financing, and putting their $50M MXN investment at risk from potential third-party claims."

strategy: "We implemented a comprehensive legal strategy based on acquisitive prescription (usucapion). We gathered documentary evidence, witness testimony, and irrefutable topographic expert reports demonstrating peaceful, continuous ownership for the time required by Oaxacan law."

outcome: "We obtained a final court judgment declaring our clients as legitimate owners. This judgment served as a deed, achieving registration at the Real Estate Registry Institute of Oaxaca (IFREO), securing the investment and enabling the tourist development project."
