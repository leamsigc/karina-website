::CaseStudyDetailContent
---
challenge: "Our client Mayra Castellanos made a property sale and when she received her new property tax bill, the amount was considerably higher than expected, creating an unexpected financial burden."

strategy: "We analyzed in detail the sale transaction and the calculations used by the municipality. We identified inconsistencies in the determination of the cadastral value and the parameters applied for tax calculation, which showed an error in the tax determination."

outcome: "We filed the corresponding administrative appeal and demonstrated to the municipal authority that there were errors in the property tax calculation. As a result, a correction was made in the tax determination, achieving that our client paid an amount that was fair and appropriate to the reality of her property, representing significant savings compared to the original bill."
