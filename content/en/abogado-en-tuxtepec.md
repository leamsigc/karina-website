## Legal Services in San Juan Bautista Tuxtepec

### Commercial Litigation for the Agricultural Sector
Tuxtepec is the agro-industrial heart of the Papaloapan Basin. Pineapple, sugarcane, rice, and citrus producers frequently face breaches of purchase agreements with packing and distribution companies. We represent cooperatives and individual producers in commercial executive proceedings, harvest collection, and compulsory enforcement of supply contracts.

### Regularization of Rural and Urban Properties
Tuxtepec's growth has left many properties — both in the city and rural areas — without proper regularization. We handle adverse possession proceedings (usucapión) for rural and urban land, subdivision titling, and measurement and boundary corrections before the civil courts of the Tuxtepec district.

### Civil and Family Law
We handle divorce proceedings, child support, child custody disputes, and testate and intestate successions. We offer close legal service, understanding the family dynamics of the Papaloapan Basin.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Services in Tuxtepec

**What do I do if a packing company won't pay me for my harvest in Tuxtepec?**
We file a commercial executive proceeding based on the purchase agreement and delivery invoices. We can request the seizure of the debtor company's assets, including bank accounts, vehicles, and machinery, to secure payment.

**How do I regularize a rural property I inherited from my parents in the Basin?**
If you have possessed the land peacefully for more than 5 years and have no title, we can file for adverse possession (usucapión). You need to prove possession with tax payment receipts, rental agreements, or neighbor testimony.

**How long does a commercial lawsuit take in Tuxtepec?**
An uncontested commercial executive proceeding can be resolved in 3 to 6 months. If the defendant raises objections and defenses, the process may extend to 12 months. In any case, the seizure is immediate.

**What do I need to start a divorce in Tuxtepec?**
Only your marriage certificate, official ID, and CURP. No-fault divorce does not require stating a cause or fault. In 2 to 4 months you obtain the dissolution of marriage judgment.

**Can I sue a tenant who won't pay rent in Tuxtepec?**
Yes. Through a lease contract rescission proceeding plus payment of overdue rent. The judge orders eviction and payment of past-due months. In Tuxtepec, these cases are usually resolved in 2 to 3 months.
