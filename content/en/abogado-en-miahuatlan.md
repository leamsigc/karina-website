## Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Services in Miahuatlan

**My family grows coffee on land without deeds. Can we lose it?**
If someone else claims ownership or if authorities detect it, there is a risk. An adverse possession proceeding gives you the legal certainty you need.

**What do I need for my mezcal to have Denomination of Origin?**
In addition to meeting production standards, you must prove ownership or legal possession of the land where the agave is grown. We help you with the legal documentation.

**How much does a succession proceeding cost in Miahuatlan?**
It depends on the number of assets and heirs. We provide a clear quote from the first consultation, with no hidden costs.

**A buyer promised to pay for my coffee after harvest and hasn't paid. What do I do?**
We can initiate a commercial enforcement proceeding if you have signed contracts or purchase orders. Recovery can happen in weeks if we secure an attachment.

**Can I sell my land if I only have a possession document?**
Legally, it is not advisable. We must first regularize it through adverse possession so you can grant proper deeds and certainty to the buyer.
