## Our Civil Law Services in Oaxaca

### Usucaption Lawsuits (Positive Prescription) in Oaxaca
**Usucaption in Oaxaca** is the legal mechanism to obtain the deed to a property that has been possessed publicly, peacefully, and continuously for the time established by the Civil Code of the State of Oaxaca. It is the solution for thousands of families who inhabit land without proper property titles.
- Urban lots in Oaxaca de Juárez and surrounding towns (Xoxocotlán, Etla, etc.).
- Rural properties in Valles Centrales and other regions.
- Inherited properties that were never formalized.

### Succession Proceedings — Intestate and Testamentary
When a family member passes away with or without a will, the heirs need a **succession proceeding in Oaxaca** to formalize the transmission of assets. Without this, real estate, vehicles, and bank accounts cannot be legally transferred.
- **Intestate Succession**: For cases without a will, we identify legal heirs and process judicial recognition.
- **Testamentary Succession**: We execute the will so that assets pass to the legitimate beneficiaries.
- Legal registration in the Public Registry of Property.

### Summary Mortgage Trial & Contract Litigation
If you are facing issues with a mortgage or need to execute one, the **Summary Mortgage Trial** (Juicio Sumario Hipotecario) is a specialized procedure in Oaxaca to resolve these matters quickly. We also represent clients in:
- Breach of contract lawsuits.
- Rescission of purchase agreements.
- Eviction trials (unpaid rent / lease termination).

### High-Rigor Civil Contract Drafting
A poorly drafted contract is a source of future conflict. We draft:
- **Property Purchase Contracts** with protective clauses for both parties.
- **Lease Agreements (Rent)** with specific penal, deposit, and rescission clauses.
- **Loan for Use (Comodato) Contracts**.
- **Notarial formalization** of agreements.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does a Usucaption trial take in Oaxaca?**
Under normal conditions, a positive prescription trial takes between **12 and 24 months**. The timeframe depends on the court's case load and if there is opposition from third parties.

**Do I need a will to protect my assets in Oaxaca?**
A will is not mandatory but highly recommended. Without it, the inheritance process is longer and more expensive, especially with multiple heirs or assets in different locations.

**Can I claim an inheritance if there was no will?**
Yes. You must initiate an **Intestate Succession Proceeding**. The law will determine the heirs based on kinship degrees (children, spouse, parents, etc.).

**What is the Summary Mortgage Trial?**
It is a fast-track legal procedure in Oaxaca specifically for the execution or defense of mortgage-backed loans. It is much faster than an ordinary civil trial.

**Can I buy a property that has no deeds?**
It is not ideal, but if you have already acquired one, Usucaption is the way to regularize it. If you are planning to buy, we recommend a **Due Diligence** check before making any payment.
