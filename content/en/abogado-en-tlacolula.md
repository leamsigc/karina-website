## Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Services in Tlacolula

**My family has had a stall at the Tlacolula market for 30 years but we have no paperwork. Can we lose it?**
There is a risk if the municipality decides to reorganize the market. An adverse possession proceeding and obtaining a municipal permit would provide legal security.

**I sell handicrafts in Tlacolula and have seen others selling my designs. Can I do anything?**
Yes. Registering your trademark and designs with IMPI is the first step. We can then send cease-and-desist letters or initiate legal action for unfair competition.

**A foreigner wants to buy my property in Tlacolula. Is it legal?**
Foreigners can acquire properties in Tlacolula without restriction (it is not a restricted zone). We need to verify the documentation and draft the appropriate contract.

**How much does it cost to regularize a commercial property in Tlacolula?**
It depends on the size and complexity of the case. From the free initial consultation, we provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

**The municipality wants to charge me a tax I consider unfair on my property. Can I challenge it?**
Yes. A nullity proceeding can be filed before the Administrative Justice Court. We evaluate whether the charge has a legal basis.
