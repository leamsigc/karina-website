## Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Services in Salina Cruz

**How can I recover unpaid invoices from a client at the port?**
The commercial executive proceeding is the most effective route. With signed contracts and invoices, we request the seizure of the debtor's bank accounts and assets. In our experience, we recover 100% of the past-due portfolio within 6 to 12 months.

**What do I need to challenge a fine from SEMARNAT or API?**
We file an annulment appeal before the Federal Court of Administrative Justice. The fine must be properly grounded and motivated. If the authority did not follow legal procedure, we obtain its annulment.

**How long does a wrongful termination labor lawsuit take in Salina Cruz?**
The process before the Conciliation and Arbitration Board can last 6 to 18 months depending on the evidentiary burden. We advise you to receive the constitutional severance of 3 months' salary plus 20 days per year worked.

**Can I sue a partner for breach of agreement in a port company?**
Yes. If there is a partnership agreement or shareholders' agreement, we can sue for compulsory performance or separation of the breaching partner before the commercial courts of Oaxaca.

**What permits do I need to operate a business inside the port zone?**
You require authorization from API Salina Cruz, plus municipal permits, Civil Protection clearance, and depending on the activity, SEMARNAT or COFEPRIS approval. We help you manage all the regulatory documentation.
