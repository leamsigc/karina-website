## Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Services in Ocotlan

**A client has owed me $80,000 MXN for a year. Can I still sue?**
Yes. Commercial debt prescribes in 10 years. We can initiate the enforcement proceeding and request an asset attachment immediately.

**I have a mezcal distillery on ejido land. Can I get deeds?**
It is possible, but it requires a process before the Unitary Agrarian Tribunal. We guide you through the entire procedure to obtain certification of your rights.

**My mother passed away without a will. There are 4 children and we cannot agree. What happens?**
A contentious intestate succession proceeding is initiated. A judge will determine the asset partition. It is better to reach an agreement to avoid prolonged costs and delays.

**How long does a divorce take in Ocotlan?**
A voluntary divorce takes 2 to 4 months. A no-fault divorce (without agreement) can take 6 to 12 months depending on the court's caseload.

**Can I sell my property if I have a purchase agreement but no deed?**
Formally, no. You need to regularize first through adverse possession to grant public deeds to the buyer. We handle the entire process.
