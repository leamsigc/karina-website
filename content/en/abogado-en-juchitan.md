## Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Services in Juchitán

**What should I review before signing a wind energy lease contract?**
You must verify the contract duration (commonly 20-30 years), the royalty percentage, automatic renewal clauses, and restrictions on the use of your land. We analyze every point before you sign.

**How can I recover a loan that someone won't pay in Juchitán?**
The commercial executive proceeding is the fastest route. With a properly signed promissory note or loan contract, we can request the seizure of the debtor's assets and forced payment before the civil court of the district.

**What if my wind energy lessee doesn't pay the agreed royalties?**
We initiate a compulsory contract enforcement proceeding. Oaxaca law protects the property owner as long as there is a registered contract. We help you claim both past-due royalties and late payment interest.

**How long does a divorce take in Juchitán?**
A no-fault divorce can be resolved in 3 to 6 months if there is no dispute over assets or children. With property conflicts, the timeline depends on the complexity of the inventory and appraisal.

**Can I sue a wind energy company for damage to my land?**
Yes. If the construction or operation of the wind farm has caused damage to your land, access roads, or water bodies, we can file a damages claim before the civil courts of Oaxaca.
